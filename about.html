About RBXY PCB

Shenzhen Ruiboxingyuan Electronics Co., Limited.

Our Company

Established in 2009 in Shenzhen, RBXY PCB focuses on high-quality multilayer and HDI PCB manufacturing. Our factory is located in Bao'an, Shenzhen, occupying an area of 12,000 square meters with a dedicated team of 180+ employees. We serve global clients across electronics, telecommunications, automotive, medical, aerospace, and consumer electronics industries.

Our Strengths

  • Advanced production facility with full-process capability
  • Professional engineering & technical team
  • Strict quality control per IPC Class 2 & 3
  • Customized PCB solutions & on-time delivery
