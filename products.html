Multilayer PCB

High-Layer, High-Frequency, High-Speed，High-Complexity PCBs.

  • • Layers: 2–40
  • • Thickness: 0.2–6.0mm
  • • Copper: 1–10oz
  • • Materials: FR-4, High-Tg FR-4, TUC, Rogers, Panasonic, Ventec
  • • Surface Finish: OSP, HAL, LF HAL, Immersion Sn/Ag, ENIG, ENEPIG, Hard Gold, Selective Hard Gold, Soft Gold

HDI PCB

High-Density Interconnect PCB with microvias, fine lines, blind/buried vias (any-layer HDI).

  • • Layers: 4–20
  • • Microvia: 0.075–0.125mm
  • • Line/Space: 3/3mil
  • • Vias: Blind, Buried, Staggered, Stacked
  • • VIPPO: Epoxy Filled and Capped Vias
